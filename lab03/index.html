Cake Tips and Techniques

Professional Tips

When baking a cheesecake, it is important to not over bake it. You only want to bake it until the middle has a slight wiggle, not until its rock solid.

It is important that you use a water bath , discussed at the right , to ensure even baking of your cheesecake .

To create a water bath, use a pan that wil allow you to fill it with boiling water that goes halfway up the spring form pan in which the cake is placed.
